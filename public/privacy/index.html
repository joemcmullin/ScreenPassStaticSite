Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 7, 2026 · Last Updated: June 7, 2026

ScreenPass (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the ScreenPass iPhone application and associated website (screenpassapp.com).

Please read this policy carefully. By using ScreenPass, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who We Are

ScreenPass is an iPhone application developed and operated by Apex Development Studio LLC, a software company registered in the Commonwealth of Virginia. ScreenPass is a parental-control and screen-time management app that helps a parent or guardian set flexible schedules and approve or deny “Pass Requests” for the children and devices they are responsible for. We are not a healthcare provider and do not collect health data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information stored on your device (the App)

The schedules you create, your children’s profiles (a name and an icon you choose), and your Pass Request history are stored on your device in a secure App Group container. This information is not transmitted to us and is not kept on our servers — the App does not require an account and does not operate a backend for this data.

ScreenPass is built on Apple’s Family Controls, Managed Settings, and Device Activity frameworks. Apple provides these through privacy-preserving opaque tokens: the App can restrict an app or website you select, but it never receives the app’s name, bundle identifier, URL, your child’s messages, photos, browsing history, or any other content. We cannot see what Apple does not give us — and Apple does not give us your child’s activity.

2.2 Information you provide through the Site

When you join the launch list or contact us through screenpassapp.com, you provide your name, email address, and any message you write. We use this solely to reply to you and, if you ask, to notify you when ScreenPass launches.

2.3 Information related to purchases

Subscriptions and the free trial are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple tells the App whether your subscription is active so we can unlock features — we never receive your name, card number, or billing details.

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

ScreenPass does not collect:

  • Your full name (beyond a first name you may include in a message)
  • Your physical address or precise location
  • Your date of birth or government-issued identifiers
  • Any health information
  • Your contacts or photos
  • Your child’s activity, content, messages, or browsing
  • Payment card information (payments are processed by the Apple App Store)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 To provide the application

We use the limited information described above to operate ScreenPass, respond to your messages and support requests, send the one launch email you requested (if you joined the list), maintain the security and integrity of the App and Site, and comply with our legal obligations.

3.2 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for any purpose, including advertising.

3.3 We Do Not Use Your Data for Advertising

ScreenPass does not display advertisements, contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and does not build behavioral profiles or track you across other apps or websites. (Because the App does not track you, it does not present an App Tracking Transparency prompt.)

4. How We Store Your Information

4.1 On your device

All App data is stored locally on your iPhone, protected by iOS’s app sandbox and App Group protections and available only while your device is unlocked.

4.2 Not on our servers

ScreenPass does not sync your family’s data to our servers or to a developer-controlled cloud. Information you submit through the Site is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) to the providers listed below.

5. Information Sharing

5.1 Apple

We use Apple’s platform services (the App Store, StoreKit for subscriptions, and the Family Controls frameworks) to operate ScreenPass. Your use of these services is subject to Apple’s Privacy Policy (apple.com/privacy). Apple is the only third party involved in the App itself.

5.2 Fernand (website contact form)

This applies only to the website. When you submit the contact or launch-list form on screenpassapp.com, your submission is transmitted to and processed by Fernand (getfernand.com), which receives the name, email address, and message content you provide so we can reply. This information is not used for advertising and is not sold. The App does not use Fernand.

5.3 No Other Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access your data

You can view all of your App data within ScreenPass at any time.

6.2 Delete your data

You can delete all App data at any time by removing the App from your device. To request deletion of information you’ve sent us through the Site, email support@screenpassapp.com.

6.3 California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used, to request correction or deletion, to opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of personal information, and to non-discrimination for exercising these rights. We do not sell or share personal information, and the App does not collect sensitive personal information. To exercise these rights, email support@screenpassapp.com; we will respond within 45 days, or notify you and respond within 90 days total if an extension is necessary.

6.4 Virginia, Colorado, and other state residents

Residents of Virginia (VCDPA), Colorado (CPA), and other states with consumer-privacy laws have similar rights to access, correct, and delete personal information and to opt out of targeted advertising and the sale of personal information — neither of which we do. Contact support@screenpassapp.com to exercise these rights.

6.5 EEA & UK residents (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA or UK, you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection, and may lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. Our legal basis for processing the information you submit is your consent and our legitimate interest in responding to you.

7. Children’s Privacy

ScreenPass is rated 4+ and is designed to be set up and operated by a parent, guardian, or other responsible adult to manage the children and devices they are responsible for. Because your family’s data stays on the device and is never transmitted to us, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. We do not direct the Site to children and do not knowingly collect information from a child under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country) through the Site. If you believe a child has provided us personal information through the Site, contact support@screenpassapp.com and we will delete it.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect information, including: on-device storage protected by the iOS app sandbox and App Group protections; encrypted transmission (HTTPS) for anything submitted through the Site; no developer-operated servers storing your family’s data; and data minimization. No security system is perfect; if we become aware of a security breach affecting your information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

9. Breach Notification

We hold almost no personal information, but in the event of a security breach affecting information you submitted through the Site, we will notify affected individuals and any applicable regulators in accordance with applicable state and federal law, within the timeframe the law requires. Notice will describe the nature of the breach, the information involved, steps you can take, and how to contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last Updated” date above and, where appropriate, note the change in the App. Your continued use of ScreenPass after an update constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle information, please contact us:

Apex Development Studio LLC
App & privacy support: support@screenpassapp.com
Business & legal: support@apexdevelopmentstudio.com
Website: screenpassapp.com
Mailing address: available upon request — contact support@screenpassapp.com

For data-deletion requests or to exercise your privacy rights, email support@screenpassapp.com with the subject line “Privacy Rights Request.”
